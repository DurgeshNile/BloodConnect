Blood Donation Awareness

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age between 18 to 65 years
  • Weight at least 50 kg
  • No recent illness or medication
  • No alcohol consumption 24 hours before donation
  • Good overall health condition

Frequently Asked Questions

When can I donate again?

You can donate whole blood every 3 months (90 days).

Is blood donation safe?

Yes, completely safe. Sterile, single-use equipment is used.

How long does donation take?

The actual donation takes 8-10 minutes, total process about 30 minutes.
